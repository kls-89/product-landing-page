Who We Are

Sidewalk Serenades is the authority for on demand street music.

We are the source for all of your spontaneous music needs. Simply pick a date, time, location, and the type of serenade that best fits your needs, and we'll do the rest!

We specialize in "impromptu" romantic music gestures, but we also work great for company outings and other large events.

Whether you want to surprise your sweetheart at dinner with a selection of love songs played on an accordion, or if you want to bring a smile to someone's face with a spontaneous Mariachi Band performance in the park -- Sidewalk Serenades has you covered!

Frequently Asked Questions:

Can I request a custom playlist?

Our musicians are happy to take requests; however, custom playlists require 24 hours advanced notice, so that our artists can learn the songs.

Where can I find a Sidewalk Serenader?

We're working diligently to increase the number of performers available in as many cities as possible. So far, we have registered artists in:

  • Boston, MA
  • Los Angeles, CA
  • New York, NY
  • Portland, OR
  • San Francisco, CA
  • Seattle, WA

I'm a musician! Can I be a Sidewalk Serenader?

We're always looking to expand our list of available performers! The more musicians across the most number of cities means the power of song is shared with more and more people.

Why Sidewalk Serenaders over anyone else?

Sidewalk Serenaders is all about bringing people together through the power of music and art. Your purchase helps our artists to make a living doing what they love best. The best artistic performances, we feel, are from those whose passion runs deep throughout their craft. And in return, the audience is left with an unforgettable and entirely unique experience.

Music is the medium through which the human experience is linked. Sidewalk Serenaders strives to foster the creative output of some for the enjoyment and love of those around them.
